Teacher Job Description

We are looking for a dedicated Teacher to deliver high-quality education in a classroom setting. Responsibilities include:
- Developing lesson plans and curricula aligned with educational standards.
- Teaching subjects such as mathematics, science, or literature.
- Assessing student performance through assignments, tests, and projects.
- Incorporating pedagogy and classroom management techniques.
- Using educational technology (e.g., smartboards, LMS like Moodle).
- Fostering critical thinking, collaboration, and creativity in students.
- Engaging with parents and school administration for student progress.

Required Skills:
- Curriculum development, pedagogy, classroom management
- Educational technology, assessment, lesson planning
- Communication, empathy, subject expertise (e.g., mathematics, science)
- Knowledge of child development and learning methodologies

Experience: 2+ years in teaching or educational instruction.
