Designer and Web Developer Job Description

We are hiring a creative Designer and Web Developer to create visually appealing and functional digital solutions. Responsibilities include:
- Designing UI/UX for websites and applications using Figma, Adobe XD, or Photoshop.
- Developing responsive websites with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks (React, Angular, Vue.js).
- Implementing SEO best practices and RESTful APIs.
- Creating graphic designs, wireframes, and prototypes.
- Managing backend with Node.js, Django, or PHP.
- Using version control (Git) and cloud platforms (AWS, Heroku).
- Collaborating with teams to align designs with brand guidelines.

Required Skills:
- UI/UX design, graphic design, wireframing, prototyping
- HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Django
- Figma, Adobe XD, Photoshop, SEO, RESTful APIs
- Creativity, attention to detail, teamwork

Experience: 2+ years in design and/or web development.
