Business Analyst Job Description

We are seeking a Business Analyst to bridge business needs and technical solutions. Responsibilities include:
- Gathering and documenting requirements using SWOT analysis and stakeholder interviews.
- Creating reports and dashboards with Excel, Tableau, or PowerBI.
- Analyzing business processes and recommending improvements.
- Querying databases with SQL for data insights.
- Supporting project management with Agile or Scrum methodologies.
- Facilitating communication between technical and non-technical teams.
- Conducting financial analysis and forecasting.

Required Skills:
- Requirements gathering, SWOT analysis, process modeling
- Excel, SQL, Tableau, PowerBI, business intelligence
- Agile, Scrum, project management, financial analysis
- Strong communication and analytical skills

Experience: 3+ years in business analysis or related fields.
